# MBrowser - A Feature-Rich Mobile Browser

## Overview

MBrowser is a mobile browser application designed to provide a seamless and efficient browsing experience on your mobile device. It comes packed with features that enhance your web exploration, including a customizable dashboard with common links and a built-in ad blocker.

## Features

### Dashboard

The dashboard is your personalized gateway to the internet, offering quick access to commonly visited websites. Customize it according to your preferences and enjoy a faster browsing experience.

### Adblocker

MBrowser comes equipped with a powerful ad blocker to enhance your web experience. Say goodbye to intrusive ads and enjoy a cleaner and faster browsing environment.
